About Me

I am a PhD candidate in Economics at the Indira Gandhi Institute of Development Research (IGIDR), Mumbai. My research interests are in agricultural economics and environmental and resource economics.

My current work studies questions around rainfall shocks, cropping intensity, adaptive measures, weather-related agricultural risk, energy poverty, and firm financial transparency.

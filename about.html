About

PhD candidate in Economics at IGIDR, Mumbai.

I am a PhD candidate in Economics at the Indira Gandhi Institute of Development Research (IGIDR), Mumbai. My research interests are in agricultural economics and environmental and resource economics.

My current work studies questions around rainfall shocks, cropping intensity, adaptive measures, weather-related agricultural risk, energy poverty, and firm financial transparency.

Education

  • PhD in Economics, Indira Gandhi Institute of Development Research (IGIDR), Mumbai, India, 2020-2026.
  • M.A. in Economics, Department of Economics, University of Hyderabad, India, 2017-2019.
  • B.A. in Economics and Psychology, University of Allahabad, India, 2014-2017.

Research Fields

Agricultural economics; environmental and resource economics.
